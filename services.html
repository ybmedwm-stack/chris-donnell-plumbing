Our Services

Master-licensed plumbing & heating, top to bottom.

Two-plus decades of working on Seacoast homes and businesses means we've seen it all — and we treat your project with the same care whether it's a faucet or a full mechanical room.

Plumbing Repair & Installation

The bread and butter. Leaky pipes, dripping faucets, running toilets, fixture replacement, garbage disposals, sump pumps, and emergency repairs. We diagnose the actual cause — not just the visible symptom — and fix it cleanly.

  • Pipe repair & replacement
  • Faucet & fixture installation
  • Toilet repair & replacement
  • Garbage disposal service
  • Sump pump installation
  • Emergency leak repair

Heating Systems & Boilers

Maine winters punish weak heating systems. We install, service, and replace boilers, furnaces, and heating distribution components for residential and commercial customers — from one-bedroom cottages to multi-unit buildings.

  • Boiler installation & replacement
  • Furnace service & repair
  • Heating system diagnostics
  • Hydronic & baseboard heat
  • Annual tune-ups
  • Hot water radiators

Water Heaters — Tank & Tankless

Cold showers are a solvable problem. We service every major brand and size the right replacement for your household — including tankless conversions that pay for themselves in space and efficiency.

  • Tank water heater install
  • Tankless water heaters
  • Heat pump water heaters
  • Repair & troubleshooting
  • Anode rod & valve service
  • Recirculation systems

Bathroom & Kitchen Plumbing

  • Pot fillers & bar sinks

Drain Cleaning & Sewer Lines
